What This Is

Jarvis OS coordinates a fleet of specialized AI agents that receive work through Discord, execute it via local LLMs (Qwen 3.5 on LM Studio), and surface results for operator review. The quant system discovers, validates, and paper-trades NQ futures strategies. Nothing reaches production without human sign-off.
